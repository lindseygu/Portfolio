Echoes of Motions

Interactive Installation/Digital Media

Achieved using MaxMSP

Role

Computing Artist, Programmer

Collaborators

None

Timeline

January 2022 - March 2022

Video



Description

A 40-second animation that explores the concept of “Time is cycling, the only passing subject is us”. By combining generative arts of ocean wave movements(using p5.js), hand-drawn animation of a clock with a figure walking, and the various natural sounds displayed by multi-channel speakers, I want to create an immersive environment for the audience to experience the relationship between human and the nature.
