Hi there. I'm Lindsey!

Yuemeng(Lindsey)Gu is a computational/interdisciplinary artist and UI/UX designer focusing on everyday experiences as evidence and inspirations for her work. She is currently double-majoring in Interdisciplinary Computing and the Arts Major and Cognitive Science with a Specialization in design & Interaction at the University of California, San Diego.

With a discovering eye for detail, she explores the relationship between humans and the natural world, capturing moments of harmony and juxtapositions using technologies like computational softwares or machine learning models. Born in China and later moved to California, her work as an international artist focuses on the intersection and merging between cultures.
